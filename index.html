SORRY

I'm Sorry, Appa

Appa,

I know I have made mistakes. I know I haven't always listened to you.

There were times when I disappointed you and hurt you. I carried that guilt for a long time, and I carry it still — not as a burden, but as a reminder to do better.

I am truly sorry.

Thank you for never giving up on me — even when I gave you every reason to. Thank you for loving me not for who I was in those moments, but for who you believed I could become.

I promise I will work hard and make you proud. I will not waste the sacrifices you made. I will become someone who deserves a father like you.

Thank you for loving me, even when I made mistakes.

I love you, Appa ❤️
Your son, Niranjan

Forever yours,
